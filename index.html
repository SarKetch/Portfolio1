Welcome to our historic haven on James Island, Charleston, South Carolina.

Built in 1875 and nestled at the end of a tranquil street, our charming property offers an idyllic setting for weddings and special events. Surrounded by grand oak trees and overlooking the serene creek system with distant views of the lighthouse, our gracious lawn and oak-covered grassy area provide the perfect backdrop for your unforgettable gatherings.

Discover a unique blend of history, nature, and modern comfort at our James Island retreat, which includes:

  • WiFi for seamless connectivity.
  • A refreshing pool for relaxation and enjoyment.
  • A fully stocked bar in the spacious pool house, ready to cater to your event needs.
  • A TV for hosting viewing parties.
